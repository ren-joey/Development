New Timer Valve Range

2/2 Timer Controlled Valves Launch, 17 August 2015
August 24, 2015
Alfanumatic 2/2 Timer Controlled Valves Launched

Alfanumatic has launched a brand new series of timer controlled valves. The ANCS range is excellent for use as auto drain valves for pneumatic condensate applications. These 2/2 way valves are available in both 220VAC as well as 24VDC with thread sizes of 1/4″, 3/8″ as well as 1/2″ (Female). Check out our download page for detailed technical specifications.
